FAQ

Answers before you get started

Still unsure if Lockin is right for you? Here are a few of the most common questions from students.

Do I need to pay to use Lockin?

No. The Starter plan is free for individual students and gives you access to study groups, chat, and basic scheduling. You only pay if you choose to upgrade to Plus or Premium.

Can I use Lockin without my university?

Yes. You can create or join groups with classmates even if your university hasn‚Äôt officially partnered with Lockin yet. Campus-wide analytics are only available on Premium.

Which platforms are supported?

Lockin works on iOS and Android. A responsive web version is also available on modern browsers so you can access your groups from your laptop.

Can teachers or TAs use Lockin?

Absolutely. Many instructors use Lockin to host review sessions and office-hours style groups. Just create a private group and share the invite link with your students.
