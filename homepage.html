🔒 Lockin

App to find the ideal study buddy or study group

Overview | Features | About | Contact

Overview

Lockin is an app designed for students who want to build effective study groups. With a simple and intuitive matching system, you can swipe to find the perfect study buddy to work with.

Main Features

  • 🔁 Swipe to find your match: swipe to accept or skip other students’ profiles.
  • 🎯 Find your ideal study buddy: discover students who share your interests and subjects.
  • 📚 Build your perfect study group: join or create a group that matches your working style.
  • 💬 Chat and plan: built-in messaging to organize your study sessions.

About the Project

This project was developed as part of a school assignment. The goal was to create an app idea that is useful for student life and that encourages collaboration, motivation, and academic success.
